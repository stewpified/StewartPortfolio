Recipe & Crafting System

DataTable-driven crafting built on FRecipeIngredient and FRecipeRow structs. TryCraftRecipe handles validation; station-gating via an EStationType enum limits which recipes are available at which station; a cached craftable-recipe list keeps lookups cheap as the recipe book grows.

Weight-Based Fish Scaling

Caught weight drives mesh scale through a cube-root formula, clamped at both ends and remapped through a Curve Float asset so art direction can override the raw math. Non-uniform scaling adds girth independent of length.
